FEATURES

BoscoTryll Features

Experience Deep Rock Galactic with an intelligent AI companion

AI Controls Game Mechanics NEW

Watch as Bosco spawns enemy waves when insulted and adjusts mineral prices based on how you treat him - all through AI decisions, not scripting.

Persistent Memory NEW

Bosco remembers how you treat him across sessions, building friendship or resentment. Your NPCs can have real memory and emotional depth.

Context-Aware Responses

Bosco understands Rock & Stone salutes vs insults. NPCs interpret player intent from any text input - no predefined commands needed.

Dynamic Voice Acting

Every Bosco line is generated in real-time. Infinite dialogue variety without recording studios or voice actor costs.

Customizable Personalities

Choose Bosco's personality: Veteran Miner, Lazy Bot, or create your own. Let players customize NPCs without breaking immersion.

SETUP

Installation Guide

Installation

  1. 1. Run installer.exe
  2. 2. Allow Windows Defender when prompted
  3. 3. Wait for automatic installation (~10-15 minutes)
    • •Python 3.12 with dependencies
    • •Ollama with qwen2.5:7b model (~6GB download)
    • •All required mod files

Video Tutorial

Usage

  1. 1. Run launcher.exe from the desktop or from BoscoTryll folder. Keep it open.
  2. 2. Launch Deep Rock Galactic
  3. 3. The mod will work automatically in any mission with Bosco

You can safely restart launcher.exe during gameplay if needed.

Share Your Personality Prompts

Share your best Bosco personality prompts with the community in our Discord!

#prompt-engineering

Edit Bosco's personality by modifying the configuration file:

Steam\steamapps\common\Deep Rock Galactic\FSD\Mods\BoscoTryll\library\bosco_config.json:

{
  "personality": "Sad and depressed personality. Very lazy and lamenting everything. Clearly not enthusiastic to be here. Every response carries some regret and complaining but reluctingly submissive.",
  "voice": "am_michael"
}

Voice Customization:

Change Bosco's voice by replacing "voice": "am_michael" with one of the available English voices.
