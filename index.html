FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Tryll Engine

What is Tryll Engine?

Tryll Engine is a complete AI infrastructure for game developers that enables intelligent NPCs, dynamic dialogues, and adaptive learning systems. It runs locally on player hardware with zero cloud costs.

How does local AI work?

Tryll Engine runs AI models directly on the player's device, eliminating the need for cloud servers. This means instant responses, complete privacy, and no ongoing costs for developers or players.

Which game engines are supported?

Currently, we support Unreal Engine with a 1-click plugin. Unity support is in development. We're also working on support for other popular engines based on community demand.

What are the system requirements?

Tryll Engine automatically optimizes for available hardware. It works on a wide range of PCs, from budget systems to high-end gaming rigs. The engine automatically selects the best AI model for each player's hardware.

Is it really free for indie developers?

Yes! If you've generated or raised less than $200k in the past 12 months, Tryll Engine is completely free. This includes all features, documentation, and Discord community support.

How do I maintain creative control?

Tryll Engine uses a hybrid approach. You can keep traditional dialogue trees visible while allowing natural language input. The AI intelligently maps player input to your predefined options, giving players freedom while maintaining your narrative control.

What about content safety?

Tryll Engine includes comprehensive content filtering to ensure AI responses stay within your creative boundaries and community standards. You have full control over what the AI can and cannot say.

Can I try it before committing?

Absolutely! We offer live demos and will have a downloadable mod for Deep Rock Galactic coming soon. You can also join our Discord community to see examples and get your questions answered.
