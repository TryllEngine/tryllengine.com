We're excited to welcome [Miloš Petković](https://www.linkedin.com/in/milos-petkovic-109a2098/) to Tryll Engine as our Tech Lead!

![Technical Lead — Miloš Petković](/assets/blog/welcome-milos-petkovic-inline-1.png)

Miloš brings a decade of experience in game development, including 6 years at [Playrix](https://www.linkedin.com/company/playrix-entertainment/) as a Lead Programmer, building gameplay systems and tools used across the studio's projects, including Homescapes. His tools helped teams ship games played by hundreds of millions of people.

We asked Miloš what he's most proud of from those ten years. His answer:

"I take great pride in writing code that is clean, efficient, and dependable, but my proudest achievement has always been the people I've had the chance to work with and lead. Helping others grow, supporting them through challenges, and making our shared journey easier and more meaningful is what I value most from my career so far."

Outside of work, Miloš is a lifelong gamer and an endurance cyclist, regularly riding over 200 km a week. The same mix of passion and discipline he brings into engineering.

We're glad to have you with us, Miloš. Welcome to Tryll Engine!
