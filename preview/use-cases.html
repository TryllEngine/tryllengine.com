/status-model

What "available" means, exactly.

Honest labeling so your eng team isn't guessing at our roadmap.

Available now

Shippable in 2026.

SLM inference, embeddings, and persistent memory (semantic search). Everything you need for dynamic dialogue, NPC memory, and reasoning agents. Running on your player's GPU.

Coming soon

TTS, STT, RAG.

Local voice synthesis, speech-to-text, and retrieval-augmented generation over game knowledge bases. On the near roadmap — months, not years.

Later

Vision, image, music.

Vision-language models, real-time image generation, and music generation. Models exist, but on-device real-time use is still maturing. Pilot-only.

Experimental

Research-grade.

Unproven concepts or heavy R&D: live voice cloning, adaptive RL opponents, emotional biometrics. Interesting, but not a promise.
