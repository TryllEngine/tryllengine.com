**We're excited to welcome [Pierre Moisan](https://www.linkedin.com/in/pierre-moisan-2431272/) to Tryll Engine!**

Tryll Engine brings **on-device AI** into games, enabling developers to run AI models directly on players' hardware and create new kinds of gaming experiences.

To help us build this future, we're thrilled to be joined by Pierre Moisan, **a pioneer of the Canadian video game industry** with **30 years** of experience building and scaling game studios.

As Tryll Engine continues to grow, Pierre will support us with strategic guidance, product insight, and deep industry connections across the North American game ecosystem.

Earlier in his career, Pierre served as Vice-President at Megatoon, the studio that later became Behaviour Interactive — today one of Canada's largest game companies. He later founded ArtMedia Studios, and joined Ubisoft to help launch the company's Quebec City studio.

Pierre then spent nine years at Frima, where he was Vice-President of Strategic & Business Affairs, helping grow the company from a small team into the largest independent game studio in Canada.

Today, Pierre is **Senior Advisor** for Creative Industries at CEIM in Montreal. As we bring on-device AI to the next generation of games, Pierre's experience will be invaluable.

Welcome to the team, Pierre!
